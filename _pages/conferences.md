## About the Conference

**Carbon Conference 2026** will bring together researchers, industry leaders, and innovators working across the full landscape of carbon science.

The meeting will highlight breakthroughs in:

- Clean energy
- Clean water solutions
- Advanced carbon materials
- Electrochemical technologies
- Modeling and artificial intelligence
- Health, environmental, and extreme-environment applications

Hosted in Charleston, the conference combines a broad technical program with the city’s historic character, coastal setting, and acclaimed food culture.

---

## Scientific Themes

Carbon Conference 2026 will cover a wide range of topics across carbon science and engineering, including:

- Carbon materials for batteries and supercapacitors
- Catalysis and electrochemical conversion
- Graphite, activated carbon, and carbon fibers
- Graphene, nanotubes, and other 2D/3D nanomaterials
- Clean water and environmental technologies
- Modeling, data science, and AI for carbon research
- Extreme-environment carbon materials
- Carbon-enabled health technologies
