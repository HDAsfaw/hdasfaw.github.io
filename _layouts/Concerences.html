About the conference

Carbon Conference 2026 will bring together researchers, industry leaders, and innovators working across the full landscape of carbon science. The meeting highlights breakthroughs in clean energy and water, advanced carbon materials, electrochemical technologies, modeling, artificial intelligence, and next-generation applications in health, environment, and extreme operating conditions.

Hosted in Charleston, the event combines a broad technical program with the historic character, coastal setting, and acclaimed food culture of one of the most distinctive cities in the southeastern United States.

Scientific themes

  • Carbon materials for batteries and supercapacitors
  • Catalysis and electrochemical conversion
  • Graphite, activated carbon, and carbon fibers
  • Graphene, nanotubes, and 2D/3D nanomaterials
  • Clean water and environmental technologies
  • Modeling, data science, and AI for carbon research
  • Extreme-environment carbon materials
  • Carbon-enabled health technologies

Why attend?

The conference is designed for discovery, exchange, and collaboration across academia and industry. Attendees can expect plenary talks from internationally recognized scientists, opportunities to present research, sponsor engagement, and updates on emerging directions in carbon science and engineering.
